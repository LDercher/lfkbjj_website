Mission

Lawrence Jiu Jitsu Mission Statement Our mission is to coach, motivate and inspire those who have chosen the Brazilian Jiu Jitsu path for positively transforming their lives. We do this by providing the highest quality Jiu Jitsu in town at the most affordable price.

Here are the principles of how we live that every day:

Our Curriculum For Lawrence Jiu Jitsu, our success has always been created on the training floor. The diverse curriculum taught by highly educated and experienced instructors creates the atmosphere that allows each and every student to achieve their goals. Our curriculum for each class is pre planned and is not just “winged”. Battle tested techniques with the finest details.

Our Instructors Lawrence Jiu Jitsu Instructors are constantly seeking ways to improve through continuous training. We believe that we cannot inspire others without leading by example. Our instructors are constantly competing, researching and seeking out and bringing in the best instruction locally and from across the country.

Our Students Our students are the core of the community at Lawrence Jiu Jitsu. Our mission is to help each student reach their full potential. We understand that each student comes in with a different goal in mind and we make sure to attend to their individual needs based on those goals. Whether your journey is for self defense, weight loss or competive jiu jitsu.

Our Facility We take pride in our facility, making it a safe, clean training environment that is constantly being maintained and updated. To our students, it often acts as a home away from home and we honor that by continuing to take great care of it.

Our Customer Service Our idea of great customer service is to honor and respect each and every person that comes through our doors. This standard is held not only by the staff but through the students as well.

Bios

Charlie Hiersche (pictured center) began his martial arts journey at 8 years old in the small town of Knob Noster, Missouri. Charlie reached the rank of Red belt/black stripe in the art of Tae-Kwon-Do before he switched to the more aggressive and practical art of wrestling in the 8th grade. He wrestled on and off throughout high school, winning many matches he was not favored to win with his Tenacious style and his will to succeed. After high school he discovered the art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in 2005 and never looked back. Charlie began his training under Renato Tavares, Ethan Day, and Jason Bircher of Kansas City Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and received his rank of Black belt on July 14th 2017. Charlie has been an active competitor all over the country his entire career and continues to compete nationally and internationally every year and is continually searching to push his students and his own Jiu Jitsu. As a veteran of multiple Cage Fights (4-2) he has trained alongside with and helped multiple UFC/Bellator veterans prepare for competition. Charlie is known for training with exclusively the best BJJ players in the area to keep his skill sharp. Outside of Jiu Jitsu Charlies mother has lived in Lawrence, Kansas his entire life where she opened “The Mad Greek” in 1988. At the age of 18 he was asked to move up to run the family business where he successfully piloted the restaurant for 10 years before ultimately deciding to sacrifice his career as a restauranteur and business man to pursue becoming a public servant. Charlie has been a career Fireman and Paramedic for the last 4 years where he continues to chase his life long pursuit of helping others.

Robert Riley is a Lawrence native with more than a decade of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu teaching experience. He was co-owner, grappling instructor and Mixed Martial Arts coach at IMS (Integrated Martial Science) and later founded the Church of Choke MMA club at Haskell University before opening the original Lawrence Jiu-Jitsu, all in Lawrence. He went on to work as the Gi Jiu-Jitsu instructor and MMA coach at Grudge Training Center in Arvada Colorado. He has returned to Lawrence to share his coaching experience and build a Jiu-Jitsu community as an instructor at the new Lawrence Jiu-Jitsu at Thatcher's Training Center.
